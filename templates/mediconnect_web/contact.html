Frequently Asked Questions

To book an appointment, you need to create an account and purchase either a subscription or tokens. Then, you can browse our hospitals, select a doctor, and choose an available time slot that works for you.

Subscriptions provide unlimited access to our network of hospitals for a fixed period. Tokens are one-time use passes that allow you to book individual appointments. Choose the option that best fits your healthcare needs.

You can cancel or reschedule appointments through your account dashboard. Please note that cancellations should be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled appointment time to avoid any penalties.
